IDENTITY
JOSH BRANDON KILPATRICK

You've found content made by a human! I'm Josh Kilpatrick. I like to contemplate and write, and for many years I've wanted to create something. I always got stuck trying to find a topic or a point. Wanting to writing to be unique and valuable, I rejected most of my ideas. "These ideas have been done before," I'd think. I've wasted years tring to get past first base.

This site is my rebellion. This site doesn't care. Because I must express my views before I die, and because I can die at any moment, I must start. This site is orderless and themeless. Save only that all the content is mine, we may find no binding them at all.
